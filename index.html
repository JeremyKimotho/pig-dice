PIG DICE GAME

The game has 2 players, playing in rounds. In each turn, a player rolls a dice as many times as he wishes. If the player chooses to ‘Hold’, then his ‘Round Score’ is added to the ‘Global Score’. However if a player does not choose to ‘Hold’ and continues to ‘Roll’ and gets a roll of 1, their ‘Round Score’ is lost and it’s the other players turn. The first player to reach 100 points on ‘Global Score’ wins the game.
